    Ways to get involved:

  • Come to a meetup!

    We meet once a month.

  • Watch the live stream!

    We want our remote engagement to be lively and meaningful.

  • Help with a development task!

    Fix typos, edit copy, design/media, etc.

  • Propose a long-form talk!

    This is a great place to give your first tech talk, practice a conference talk, or speak on a common interest.

  • Propose a newsletter article!

    This can be a short-form blog or a formal article.

  • Get creative!

    Whether your interest is photography or underwater basket weaving, if you can dream up a way to incorporate it with Python, we're interested.

  • Support us! We are a non-profit and would love venue, food, in-kind, or financial support.

  • Support PyLadies Global!

    Link for PyLadies donations on PSF.
